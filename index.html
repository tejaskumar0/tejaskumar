Projects

Prime Pioneers
Prime Pioneers is a platform dedicated to bridging the digital divide for the elderly. The platform focuses on teaching essential digital skills to empower seniors to navigate the modern digital world. Through interactive lessons, hands-on practice, and supportive resources, the platform helps elderly users confidently perform tasks such as communicating with loved ones through social media, managing online banking, scheduling appointments, and accessing essential services. By promoting digital literacy, Prime Pioneers aims to foster independence, inclusion, and a better quality of life for seniors in an increasingly tech-driven society.

UniMatch
UniMatch is a social networking app crafted specifically for university students to connect with peers who share similar interests and passions. Designed to enhance the campus experience, the app enables students to form study groups, discover events, and participate in clubs and activities aligned with their academic and personal goals. With features like interest-based matchmaking, discussion forums, and real-time event notifications, UniMatch fosters a sense of community, collaboration, and networking opportunities among students. It's an all-in-one solution to bridge the gap between academics and campus social life.
